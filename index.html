The Format

This isn't another conference.
It's a conversation.

An unconference is participant-led. You shape the agenda. You learn from each other. No experts on stage. Just founders in the arena.

💬

Participant-Led

The agenda emerges from the room. Bring your burning questions, your hard-won lessons, your half-formed ideas.

🌱

Small Circles

8-30 people per session. Intimate enough for real conversation. Diverse enough for unexpected insights.

🔒

Chatham House Rules

Share what you learned, not who said it. This creates space for the unfiltered truths that actually help.
